input: What is the difference between the different types of CBD oil?

There are many different types of CBD oil. Our products are made from industrial hemp, which is a type of cannabis that contains less than 0.3% THC by dry

input: Industrial hemp is a legal plant that the USDA defines as any Cannabis sativa L. variety containing THC concentrations of no more than 0.3%. This means the plant produces little to no THC, the phyto-cannabinoid found in marijuana


that causes a feeling of intoxication.
